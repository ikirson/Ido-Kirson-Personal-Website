About Me

Ido Kirson is a senior at the Cambridge Rindge and Latin School (CRLS) interested in studying mathematics and statistics. He has lived in Cambridge his whole life. His other academic interests include economics, comparative politics, and Arabic language. Outside of school, Ido enjoys exploring the great outdoors through hiking, biking, skiing, and rowing.
